NEETsynapse Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

NEETsynapse ("we", "our", or "the app") is a NEET PG exam preparation application developed by Dr. Reetu Goyal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address via Google OAuth.
  • Quiz and performance data: Your quiz sessions, scores, XP points, streak counts, and bookmarked questions are stored to power your progress tracking and leaderboard ranking.
  • Device token: A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token is stored to send daily streak reminder notifications.
  • Analytics data: Firebase Analytics uses an Advertising ID solely for app performance analytics — not for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile, level badges, and progress analytics within the app.
  • To maintain your ranking on the leaderboard.
  • To send daily streak reminder push notifications (you may disable these in device Settings at any time).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs via Firebase Analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is processed by Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Analytics, Cloud Messaging) as our service provider, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Firebase security rules restrict data access so each user can only read or write their own data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at reetug2906@gmail.com. You may also revoke Google Sign-In access from your Google Account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

NEETsynapse is intended for users aged 18 and over (MBBS graduates and NEET PG aspirants). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
